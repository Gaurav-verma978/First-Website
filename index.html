THE FOUNDER OF BMW

BMW, or Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, was founded by Karl Rapp and Gustav Otto in 1916. While their individual companies merged to form BMW, the foundation date is recognized as March 7, 1916, the date of the merger of Gustav Otto's Flugmaschinenfabrik into Bayerische Flugzeug-Werke AG (BFW).
